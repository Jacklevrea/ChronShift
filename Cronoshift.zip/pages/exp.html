Expeditions We Offer

Carnotaurus Hunt - $8,000

Our Carnotarus is our upertier hunt. Coming in at $8,000 this expedition is sure to give you a wow factor. The Carnotarus is a colossal beast and is an astounding site for any to see. Hunting this beast won’t be easy however and is recommended for only top dog hunters. Do you have what it takes to hunt down this prehistoric monster?

Brachiosaurus Hunt - $4,000

Our Brachiosaurus is our cheapest hunt. Coming in at $4,000 this expedition will give you a taste of the thrill without being in too much danger. The Brachiosaurus is a great choice for those just getting into prehistoric hunting, and is also our CEO’s personal pick as well.

Trex Hunt - $10,000

Our T-rex is our most expensive flagship hunt. Coming in at $10,000 this expedition is the cream of the crop. The infamous tyrannosaur rex is sure to spark adrenaline into any hunter who is bold enough to chase after such a beast. This hunt requires the most planning and time however and takes three days to get set up before the client is able to hunt the beast. A deposit of $5,000 will need to be made before the hunt.

Beipiaosaurus Hunt - $7,000

Our Beipiaosaurus is our midtier hunt. Coming in at $7,000 this expedition is one for the skilled. The Beipiaosaurus is a nimble yet dangerous creature. Having a bird like appearance this prehistoric creature is sure to be a sight to see. This expedition is great for new to moderate hunters as it comes at a challenge hunting this wild beast, but does not pose a large threat.
