Our Policys

Rule 1 - Do Not Obstruct Nature

Protective suits will be issued and required for the entire duration of the trip. As for concern for the timeline of humanity and nature you will be required to stay on the designated path provided by your guide. If you do not follow their instructions, guides have full rights to take you back to the present day and are not required to issue a refund. We take procaussion in the safety of you the client but the environment and repercussions of destroying nature as well.

Rule 2 - No Trophy Collecting

Collecting any piece of the animal that is killed is prohibited. Not only the animal but taking rocks, sticks, bugs, or any other sort of wildlife is prohibited as well. As to not interfere with the timeline, clients are allowed to take a photo next to their kill and that is all. If you would like merchandise to remember your trip here at Time Safari Inc. we have a merchandise shop in the main lobby.

Rule 3 - Hunts Must Stay Confidential

For legal reasons, what goes on inside of a hunt must stay confidential. You are allowed to share and tell what you have hunted but are not allowed to disclose the information of how you got there and why. If you are found to be disclosing this information after being prompted not too Time Safar Inc. will take full legal action against you.
